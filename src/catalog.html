What do you need to do to get a business card?
1. Checkout on the website
You need to decide on the type of smart business card, add a product to the cart and pay the full cost of the product (we work on a one hundred percent advance payment). After successful payment, you will be asked to create your electronic business card, where you can already fill in all your contact details and links to social networks.
2. Design (for personal and individual business cards)
Then we will contact you and discuss with you your preferences for the layout and also ask you to send a photo / logo of the company to pedido@lobbox.es Next, we will develop it, then we will coordinate the finished business card prototype with you. After approval of the layout, we send it to print.
3. Manufacturing and programming
On average, the production and programming time is up to 7 days from the moment the layout is approved. The universal business card is sent the next day after ordering.
4. Delivery
Within Spain (2-3 days): 2€
Around the world (10-14 days): Calculated individually depending on the place of delivery

Frequently Asked Questions [FAQ]
What smartphones are compatible with CF. Business Card? Do I need an app?

Almost all smartphones with Android operating system older than 2014 are compatible with CF business card scanning. No special apps are required for Android devices. Typically, Android's nfc reader is located in the center of the back cover of the phone. Before use, you need to enable the nfc function. Scanning a QR code with a camera is also supported.

IOS - iPhone XS, XS Max, XR, 11, 11 Pro, 11 Pro Max, 12, 12 Pro, 12 Pro Max are compatible with CF Business Card and no special apps required. For these models, the nfc reader is located to the right of the rear camera. The QR code can be read through the smartphone camera.

Earlier models of iPhone X, iPhone 8 (plus), iPhone 7 (plus), iPhone SE and iPhone 6S (plus) with 14 IOS installed can read the CF.card signal via the built-in NFC reader, which must be added to the control center. Also for these iPhone models (except SE and 6S), if 14 IOS is not installed, you can download a special application. A link to additional software is sent upon request. And iPhone 6, 5, 4, etc. support scanning a QR code, which also leads to an electronic business card, through a smartphone camera or special applications for scanning a QR code.

What magic is used to transfer information to the phone? NFC!

It's all about the phone, which generates a magnetic field around itself, and when it hits, an induction current appears on the antenna of the business card. The current is collected in a capacitor and powers the microcircuit. A smart business card gives an answer to a reader (in particular, a phone) through the same antenna, transmitting the information recorded in the business card. This technology is called NFC (near field communication). Apple / Google Pay also works on the basis of NFC. Basically, you use this technology every day! And we rethought it a little and adapted it to business cards

What are the production and delivery times for CF. business cards?

The production of standard models usually takes up to 5 business days. The exact time is discussed with the manager individually when placing an order.

It can take up to 7 business days to create a custom CF. business card from the moment the layout is approved by the designer.

Standard delivery time is 2-3 days in Spain; 10-14 business days worldwide.

Can the chip get demagnetized?

The manufacturer of our NFC chips gives an unlimited warranty for their chips, but if the chip suddenly gets demagnetized, we will make a new business card for you!
