What do you need to do to get a business card?
1. Checkout on the website
You need to decide on the type of smart business card, add a product to the cart and pay the full cost of the product (we work on a one hundred percent advance payment). After successful payment, you will be asked to create your electronic business card, where you can already fill in all your contact details and links to social networks.
2. Design (for personal and individual business cards)
Then we will contact you and discuss with you your preferences for the layout and also ask you to send a photo / logo of the company to pedido@lobbox.es Next, we will develop it, then we will coordinate the finished business card prototype with you. After approval of the layout, we send it to print.
3. Manufacturing and programming
On average, the production and programming time is up to 7 days from the moment the layout is approved. The universal business card is sent the next day after ordering.
4. Delivery
Within Spain (2-3 days): 2€
Around the world (10-14 days): Calculated individually depending on the place of delivery
